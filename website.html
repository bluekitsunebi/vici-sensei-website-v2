WHY WE ARE DIFFERENT

The Three-Pillar System

My own structured, three-pillar system designed specifically for analytical minds who need clear rules to master Japanese.

PILLAR 01

The Structural Pillar

In the live sessions we use a structure-first approach, breaking down Japanese grammar into a precise, logical system with clear rules of construction. You will understand exactly how sentences are built so you can create your own correct sentences from scratch.

PILLAR 02

The Practice Pillar

We put theory into practice by solving exam papers (JLPT) in their entirety. I guide you step-by-step through complex reading and listening sections, dissecting your mistakes and explaining the exact logic behind correct answers until your execution is flawless.

PILLAR 03

The Grinding Pillar

Our live session time is too valuable to spend repeating words. You use our dedicated vocabulary app (built on a spaced repetition system) to learn words and Kanji on your own, efficiently. This ensures long-term retention with optimal daily effort.

THE ORIGIN STORY

My 16-Year Journey

The story of a passionate anime fan who bypassed traditional limitations to attain true fluency in Japanese.

2010 - 2015
🌸

The Spark

Fell completely in love with anime and watched hundreds of series even before beginning my journey.

2015
💡

Going Self-Taught

I tried attending all sorts of Japanese classes at first, but figuring out the pace was too slow for my motivated self, I quit each one after a few weeks. I decided to learn on my own, combining internet research with daily real-world conversations with native speakers.

2016
✈️

The Japan Breakthrough

At the age of 18, I was blessed with a gift that changed everything: my first trip to Japan. I swore to myself I would study even harder and go there again.

2018
💼

The Tourist Guide

Started working as a tour guide and interpreter for Japanese visitors, putting my conversational skills to the ultimate live test.

2018
🎤

Starting as a Teacher

Started teaching Japanese in my spare time, passing on the conversational shortcuts I used to break through.

2019
📺

The "Tokyo TV" Moment

Having my Japanese level acknowledged by the tourism agency I was collaborating with, I was recommended for a Tokyo TV interview and thus was featured on Japan's national television.

2020
💻

The IT Stage

Started working as a software programmer (and continued for 3 years), during which I directly collaborated with native Japanese speakers on software development projects.

2021
🎓

The Academic Reality Check

Graduated from a university's Japanese-English department, which unfortunately didn't contribute whatsoever to improving my Japanese. It completely confirmed to me that the traditional academic system is broken.

2023
🌍

Going Full-Time

I started my own company, transitioning to making a full-time living solely by teaching Japanese online.

2025
🚫

Rejecting Academia

Being offered a prestigious position to become a university teacher of the Japanese language, I gracefully turned it down. I chose to focus entirely on my current students and my path as an independent teacher.

2025
🏯

Living the Dream

Spent 3 beautiful months living in Japan while working remotely, alongside my wife.

NOW
🔥

Continuing the Mission

Having been studying Japanese for over 11 years and teaching it for 8, I continue to teach Japanese to students all around the world, bringing them from total beginners to full-fledged speakers of the language.
